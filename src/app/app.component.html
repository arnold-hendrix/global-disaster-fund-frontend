Apply for funding

Representatives of non-profits can apply for funding by registering the non-profits they represent. All that is required is an existing user account. If you are yet to register for an account you can register here. Note that every non-profit can be registered only once. Find out more about the registration process here.

Make a donation

Donations make our mission possible. All proceeds go to the non-profits that apply for funding through our website. We welcome and encourage everyone to consider donating to our cause. The contribution you make will help save lives and relocate individuals who have been impacted by climate disasters. Find out more about how you can get involved here.
